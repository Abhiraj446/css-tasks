    About

    Crystal World

    A unit of Himalaya Eco Park & Resorts, We are a happy park nestled in the holy city of mighty Ganges, Haridwar; Uttarakhand. Spread luxuriously in more than 18 acres, we have 39+ water & thrill experiences in the Amusement & the renowned ‘Water World’ Park along with one of a kind museums and multi cuisine Food court. Recently launched is 60 feet high Madness Tower with 06 water-thrill experiences that can only be enjoyed by the real Daredevils.
